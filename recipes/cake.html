Chocolate Cake

This recipe of chocolate cake is very easy, with simple ingredients and it is a very good snack to eat with a good cofee!

So, let's bake it?

Ingredients:

  • Two cups of Wheat Flour
  • Three tablespoons of butter
  • One tea cup of milk
  • One and a half cups of Sugar
  • Two eggs
  • A tablespoon of baking powder
  • A cup of chocolate powder

Steps:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl except for the baking powder.
  2. Mix everything together until the dough is smooth.
  3. Add the the baking powder and mix slowly until it is completely absorbed into the dough.
  4. Place the dough in a cake tin greased with butter and a little bit of flour,
  5. Oven time! Now take the dough to the oven at low heat.
  6. Do not open the oven before 20 minutes because spoils the baking powder magic!
  7. To check if the cake is ready, stick a knife in the dough, when the knife comes out clean you can take it out of the oven, the cake is ready to go!

Tips:

Now use your creativity to cover the cake with a syrup that is gonna make your cake beautiful and even more delicious!

You can put granulated sprinkles or peanut powder as a topping! Try it out!
